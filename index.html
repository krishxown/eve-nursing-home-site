About Eve's Nursing Home & Polyclinic

Founded on the Vision of Quality Healthcare

Established in June 1996 by the late Gouranga Paul, Eve's Nursing Home & Polyclinic has been a cornerstone of healthcare in North Kolkata for nearly three decades. Our commitment to providing quality, affordable healthcare has made us a trusted name in the community.

Under the current leadership of Mr. Gouri Sankar Paul and Mr. Goutam Paul, we continue to uphold our founder's vision while embracing modern medical practices and technology.
