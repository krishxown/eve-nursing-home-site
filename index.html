About EVE'S CLINIC

A Legacy of Healthcare Excellence Since 1996

Our Foundation

Founded in June 1996 by Late Gouranga Paul, EVE'S CLINIC NURSING HOME & POLYCLINIC has been serving the community of Dum Dum, Kolkata with unwavering commitment to healthcare excellence for over 29 years.

Our Mission

To provide comprehensive, affordable, and compassionate healthcare services to our community, maintaining the highest standards of medical care while ensuring accessibility to all.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted healthcare provider in North Kolkata, known for our clinical excellence, patient-centric approach, and innovative medical solutions.
